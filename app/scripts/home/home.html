Welcome to John Daly & Sons. We are a family run business established in 1935 based in Letterkenny, Co. Donegal.

We are third generation craftsmen who specialise in manufacturing the finest quality headstones, monuments, altars & worktops.

As experienced monumental sculptors, we provide you with a sympathetic and personal service, working with the client to ensure that you receive an appropriate memorial for your loved one.

Opening Hours:

  • 9.30am - 5.30pm Monday to Friday
  • Saturday, Sunday and after 17.30 - by appointment

We provide a variety of services which range from:

  • Headstones and memorials
  • Renovation and stone cleaning
  • Inscriptions
  • Stone kitchen worktops (Supplied and fitted)
  • Church altars (new and renovation work)
  • Statuary, plaques, markers, house names, housing estate names on boulders, hearths
